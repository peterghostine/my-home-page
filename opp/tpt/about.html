The Dunnski Family

dominickdunn55@gmail.com
@thepeoplestournament

We're Dominick Dunn and Jennifer Berzinski, two avid pool players who truly love the game and its players. We're also the producers of the People’s Tournament, a uniquely enjoyable competitive setting designed to push the limits of what’s possible. Driven by our desire to give back to the pool community, this tournament is our vision of what pool tournaments could truly be like when the focus is, first and foremost, on the players themselves. Equally, we strive to create value for our awesome sponsors and hosting venues, knowing full well that our wonderful event wouldn't be possible without them.

At the People's Tournament, competition and fun go hand in hand. That's why our main goal is to always produce a rewarding, exciting, and memorable experience for everyone involved. After all, The People's Tournament isn't just any tournament, it’s a party!

We also strive to be fully transparent with the financial aspect of our event. We believe that you, the player, have the right to know exactly where your money is going. That's why all payouts and administrative fees will always be clearly stated in our marketing material, including our flyers and social media posts. Please note that we're currently guaranteeing a 100% auction payout, and we're only charging a $10 administrative fee per entry to help cover the administrative overhead of running an event of this magnitude. All that said, rest assured we work tirelessly with our sponsors from both inside and outside our industry to produce an unparalleled experience and ensure that their generosity makes all the difference. In return, we shine a bright light on their innovative products so that you, our pool community, are enticed to support them by purchasing their products, just like they consistently support all of us!

We look forward to seeing you at the next tournament!
